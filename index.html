How We Met

It all started on a rainy autumn afternoon. Both seeking shelter from the downpour at a quiet coffee house, we ended up sharing the last vacant table. What started as a polite conversation about coffee blends turned into hours of shared laughter, dreams, and a deep connection that changed our lives forever.

The Proposal

Three years later, Zack planned a surprise trip back to the exact same mountain trail they hiked on their first anniversary. As the sunset turned the skies gold, Zack knelt down and asked Zoey to spend the rest of her life with him. Amidst happy tears, she said a thousand times, "Yes!"
